A little bit about me:

I grew up in the nestled town of La Canada, California and discovered my love for the outdoors. As an Eagle Scout from NASA’s JPL Troop, I spent countless hours hiking, running, and exploring the beautiful national parks of California. When I was 12, I began learning guitar through online YouTube lessons. I’ve enjoyed playing in various bands and recently began learning to play the drums. Another passion of mine is working on cars. During my high school summers, I honed my mechanical skills working at an auto mechanic shop and dedicated my free time rebuilding a 1994 Jeep YJ with my dad - a project that I cherish deeply.

At Berkeley, I enjoy keeping myself active and involved. I’m a member of the Division II ACHA Berkeley Men’s Ice Hockey Team. Off the ice, I serve as the alumni chair for the Alpha Tau Omega Fraternity and enjoy being part of the Computer Science Undergraduate Association and Ski Club.

Thanks for taking the time to visit my site and getting to know me a bit!
